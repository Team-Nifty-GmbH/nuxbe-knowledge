# Creating an Article

1. Click the **+** button in the top right corner next to the **Wissensdatenbank** heading.

   ![Plus button to create a new article](../screenshots/02-neuer-artikel-button.png)

2. The creation form opens with the following fields:
   - **Titel** (Title): The article title.
   - **Kategorien** (Categories): Assign the article to one or more categories.
   - **Inhalt** (Content): The article text. The editor provides formatting options such as bold, lists, links, tables and images.
   - **Dateianhänge** (File Attachments): Upload PDFs, documents or other files to be available as downloads.
   - **Veröffentlicht** (Published): Determines whether the article is visible.

   ![Empty creation form](../screenshots/03-artikel-erstellen-formular.png)

3. Fill in the fields.

   ![Filled form with example data](../screenshots/04-artikel-formular-ausgefuellt.png)

4. Click **Speichern** (Save).

   ![Save button highlighted](../screenshots/05-artikel-speichern.png)

The article is saved and displayed in the reading view.

![Saved article in view mode](../screenshots/06-artikel-ansicht.png)

> **Note:** You can also create an article directly within a specific category. Click the **+** button next to the category name in the sidebar.
